Who We Are

Alture & Co. is a discreet advisory firm based in Dubai.

Our expertise lies in creating strategic portfolio structures that bridge traditional real estate with emerging digital assets, a dual approach that provides both stability and forward positioning.

Structured around real estate and digital capital, the firm serves an international clientele between Europe and the UAE.

We maintain a deliberately limited client base, ensuring personalized attention and the highest level of service for each portfolio we manage.

Every engagement is approached with long-term vision, clarity, and full confidentiality.

What We Manage

Real Estate

Property portfolios composed of 20 to 40+ assets.

Each property is reviewed individually based on location, view, exposure, current value, and market cycles.

Coordination with selected agents and clear strategy defined for each asset.

The Problems We Solve

Investors managing multiple properties get overwhelmed by cold calls, outdated info, and agents who promise but don't deliver.

We act as your single trusted contact, coordinating only proven agents with clear strategies for each asset.

Digital Assets

Structuring of crypto portfolios: cold wallet configuration, asset organisation, and positioning aligned with UAE residency.

The Problems We Solve

Many clients rely on outdated or insecure platforms, facing limited trading hours and unclear exit strategies.

We secure portfolios with cold wallets and provide strategic asset management aligned with residency needs.
